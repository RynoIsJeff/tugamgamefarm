About Us

Your Ideal Venue & Game Farm Experience

Tugam Cattle & Game Farm, located just outside Pongola, offers a unique blend of stunning event facilities and authentic farm experiences. Whether you're planning a birthday, conference, special occasion, or simply a relaxing getaway, we provide the perfect setting surrounded by natural beauty and farm life.

From quiet mornings in nature to evenings around the boma, Tugam is a place to reconnect — with people, with the outdoors, and with the simple moments that matter.
