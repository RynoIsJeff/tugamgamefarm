Ethical Fair Chase Hunting

We promote ethical fair chase hunting. All hunting is on foot with minimum disturbance to the game and environment.

Please note: We do not allow giraffe, waterbuck, ostriches or reedbuck to be hunted. A fine of R20,000 will be charged if hunted.

Quick Notes

  • Hunting must take place at least 200m from a vehicle
  • No alcohol allowed prior to or during a hunt
  • A tracker must be present at all times
  • A wounded animal is charged at full tariff

Hunting Rules & Safety

  • Hunting must take place at least 200m from a vehicle.
  • No alcohol allowed prior to or during a hunt.
  • A tracker must be present at all times throughout the hunt and any travels on the farm.
  • A wounded animal is charged at full tariff.
  • Ensure your weapons are safe at all times.
  • Please be responsible with littering and smoking to prevent veld fires.
  • Staff may not accept cash/gratuities — gratuities go to the Farm Manager to be shared.
  • Report any problems so we can rectify what’s needed.

Neither management nor parties pertaining to the farm accept responsibility for damages, accidents, loss of life or property during your stay.
